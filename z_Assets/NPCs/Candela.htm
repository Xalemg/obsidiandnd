      Kirk, Knight of Thorns
      
                          
      Demon in Thorns
      
                          
      In my stride
      I take and divide
      Flesh and Bone
      All as hard as stone
      Each limb grows taunt
      As I hear the spirit haunt
      Yell, laugh and scream
      All a beautiful dream
      Demons break free
      A endless killing spree
      Gods try to contain
      All a great pain
      I take my place on the field
      Slaying men with untold zeal
      My armor wrapped in thorns
      Of a million rose's born
      Tearing flesh from the dregs
      Who lit the kegs
      My barbed sword
      Tears through a multitude of hoards
      Seeking the flesh of Lords
      Only to be repelled with a few words
      As men made of stone charge
      Through hordes of demons they barge
      Then a demon the size of a building
      Made them flee like children
      It appeared like a huge centipede
      And seemed to impede
      The holy warriors of Gwyn
      Dead now was the King
      Left alone on the battlefield they fled
      Many calling it their bed
      The Demon War was over
      I was a mere leftover
      Kirk, Knight of Thorns
